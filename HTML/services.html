The Artist

Jessica has enjoyed drawing and animals from an early age. She has been painting for pleasure for 15 years as she pursued a career in the animal welfare field but in 2011 she decided to combine the two things she loves most and start painting pet portraits professionally. A self taught artist, she has always had a connection with animals and tries to show that in each painting she does. Painting people's pets is truly a pleasure for her and she can't wait to hear from you!
